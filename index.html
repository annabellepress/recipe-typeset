Pizza Soup

The biggest hit ever among the Press children and their friends.
Transcribed so the recipe could be taught in cooking class.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 bags of pizza dough (or make it from scratch here!)
  • 1/2-1 lb provolone cheese (sliced)
  • 1-2 cans Campbell's Cream of Tomato Soup
  • butter or margarine for greasing bowls
  • 4-8 small glass bowls
  • cookie sheets
  • a parent or "really big" sibling!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450ÂºF
  2. Roll out dough to 1/4" in thickness
  3. Place the mouth of the bowl onto the dough (upside down) and cut 1/2" around the edge. Make 1 for each bowl.
  4. Grease glass bowls and put upside down on cookie sheets. Drape the dough circles over the bowl.
  5. Bake for 7 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Take off the dough bowl from the glass bowl and put right side up on the cookie sheet.
  7. Line the inside of the dough bowl with overlapping cheese.
  8. Bake for 3-5 minutes to melt the cheese. During this time, heat up the tomato soup.
  9. Place the dough bowl inside a regular bowl for stability and pour in the soup.
  10. Serve!
